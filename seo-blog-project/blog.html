What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving a website so it ranks higher in search engines like Google.

Types of SEO

  • Technical SEO
  • On Page SEO
  • Off Page SEO

Why SEO Matters

SEO helps websites gain organic traffic and improve visibility in search engine results.
